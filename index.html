Langki

Langki facilitates the creation of digital flashcards for the Anki spaced repetition system by fetching dictionary information from Wiktionary and keeping track of the user's vocabulary, recommending which words to learn next. I use AI to form sentences from the user's vocabulary for personalized listening practice.

Tech Stack: Java, ContentProvider Android API, OpenAI API, Gradle.
