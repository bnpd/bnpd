LanGam

I created a language learning game aspiring to refresh the concept 'choose your own adventure' books for the 21st century. The learner impacts the plot by their conversations with the (AI powered) game characters.

Langki

Langki facilitates the creation of digital flashcards for the Anki spaced repetition system by fetching dictionary information from Wiktionary and keeping track of the user's vocabulary, recommending which words to learn next. I use AI to form sentences from the user's vocabulary for personalized listening practice.

TravelOrger

TravelOrger is a browser extension that helps users organize their multi-stop trips by saving flights and trains to their Google calendar. It can calculate the cheapest and most convenient routes to see multiple cities.
